OPERATING REGULATIONS

ARTAUCTION ONLINE ARTWORK AUCTION PLATFORM

 

- Pursuant to the Civil Code 2015;

- Pursuant to the Commercial Law 2005;

- Pursuant to the Law on Electronic Transactions 2005;

- Pursuant to the Law on Asset Auction 2016;

- Pursuant to Decree No. 52/2013/NĐ-CP dated May 16, 2013 of the Government on e-commerce and its amendments, supplements, and implementation guidelines;

- Pursuant to the functions, tasks, and operating model of the ArtAuction Project under Duy Tan University;

The ArtAuction Project Management Board hereby promulgates the Operating Regulations of the ArtAuction Online Artwork Auction Platform as follows:

I. GENERAL PRINCIPLES

1. These Regulations govern the organization, management, operation, and exploitation of the ArtAuction Online Artwork Auction Platform (hereinafter referred to as "ArtAuction Platform" or "ArtAuction"), including on the Website and Mobile Application platforms.

2. These Regulations apply to all organizations and individuals participating in registration, using services, conducting auction transactions, and related activities on the ArtAuction Platform.

3. The ArtAuction Platform is organized, built, managed, and operated by the ArtAuction Project Management Board under Duy Tan University.

4. All auction activities on the ArtAuction Platform must comply with Vietnamese laws on e-commerce, asset auction, intellectual property, electronic transactions, and related legal regulations, while also complying with these Regulations and other internal regulations of the ArtAuction Platform.

5. In case the content of these Regulations differs from current legal regulations, the legal regulations shall apply.

II. MEMBER REGULATIONS

1. Members of the ArtAuction Platform are organizations and individuals who create accounts and are approved by the Platform, granted access to and use of functions and services on the system according to conditions and terms prescribed by the ArtAuction Platform.

1.1. Seller

a) A Seller is an organization or individual with full civil legal capacity and civil act capacity under the law, legally owns the artwork registered for auction, and is approved by the ArtAuction Platform as a member.

b) The Seller is responsible for performing electronic identity verification (eKYC) according to the ArtAuction Platform's procedures, including but not limited to providing Citizen ID Card (CCCD)/Passport information, facial verification, and other information (if required) to activate selling and listing rights.

c) The Seller assumes full legal responsibility and responsibility to the ArtAuction Platform for the truthfulness, accuracy, and legality of information and documents provided; for the origin, ownership, and authenticity of artwork put up for auction.

1.2. Bidder

a) A Bidder is an organization or individual with a need to participate in auctions and meets all conditions according to legal regulations and those of the ArtAuction Platform.

b) The Bidder must register an account, provide complete and truthful information as required by the system, and link a valid payment method as instructed by the ArtAuction Platform.

c) Before participating in bidding at Auction Rooms, the Bidder must ensure that the available balance in the Electronic Wallet (Wallet) on the system meets the minimum deposit amount as specifically regulated for each auction session.

III. TRANSACTION AND VERIFICATION PROCESS

1. Asset registration for auction

The Seller registers artwork on the system by uploading detailed images, descriptions, technical specifications (dimensions, material, year of creation, preservation condition, origin) and documents proving ownership and legal use rights (if any) as instructed by the ArtAuction Platform.

2. Automatic verification using artificial intelligence technology (AI Verification)

a) The ArtAuction Platform system applies artificial intelligence (AI) technology to scan and analyze images and data related to the artwork, to assist in detecting risks, copyright violations, content issues, as well as identifying signs of AI-generated artwork.

b) The system may automatically issue warnings if it detects that the artwork shows signs of being an AI-generated product, has risks of infringing copyright or related rights, or has content inconsistent with legal regulations and internal regulations of the ArtAuction Platform.

c) The system automatically assists in classifying painting themes (such as: landscape, portrait, daily life, folk art, abstract, etc.) and other technical criteria for management, search, and display purposes.

3. Approval of artwork for auction

a) Artworks that pass the automatic AI screening and filtering will be forwarded to the Administration Department/Administrator (Admin) of the ArtAuction Platform for final review and approval before creating and opening an Auction Room.

b) In cases where the AI system rejects or issues warnings but the Seller believes there is an error, the Seller has the right to submit an Appeal request, accompanied by documents and evidence proving the origin, authenticity, and ownership of the artwork, for the Administration Department to review and manually re-examine.

c) The final decision on whether to allow the artwork to be put up for auction belongs to the ArtAuction Platform.

4. Organization and implementation of online auction

a) Auction sessions are organized online, conducted in real-time on the ArtAuction platform.

b) Bidders participating in the auction place bids according to bid increments, time, and specific conditions announced for each auction session. All bids are automatically recorded by the system in real-time.

c) When the auction time ends, the system determines and records the Bidder with the highest valid bid as the winning bidder, and simultaneously notifies the result to relevant parties according to the ArtAuction Platform's internal regulations.

IV. PAYMENT AND DELIVERY POLICY

1. Payment

a) The winning bidder is responsible for making full and timely payment of the auctioned asset value as notified by the system, through:

- Linked online payment gateway (e.g., MB Bank) as instructed by the ArtAuction Platform; or

- Balance in the Electronic Wallet (Wallet) on the ArtAuction system.

b) The amount the Buyer must pay includes: Final winning bid price (Final Price) + Commission and service fees of the ArtAuction Platform (if any) + Shipping and insurance fees (if any and according to agreements/fee schedules applicable at the time of transaction).

2. Payment guarantee mechanism

a) The winning bidder's payment amount is temporarily held by the ArtAuction system for a certain period according to internal regulations and/or specific notifications for each auction session.

b) This amount will only be disbursed and transferred to the Seller after:

- The Buyer confirms receipt of the asset as described and in the agreed condition; or

- The complaint period expires according to regulations without valid complaints or disputes from the Buyer.

3. Shipping and asset delivery

a) The Seller is responsible for organizing packaging, safe preservation, and delivering the asset to the shipping unit or directly to the Buyer according to the agreed method or as instructed by the ArtAuction Platform.

b) The Seller must update the tracking number and shipping information on the ArtAuction system so the Buyer can track the delivery status.

c) In case of loss or damage to the asset during shipping, the parties shall handle it according to agreements, legal regulations, and/or specific policies and terms published on the ArtAuction Platform.

V. IMPLEMENTATION PROVISIONS

1. These Regulations take effect from the date of official publication on the ArtAuction Platform and replace all previous regulations and guidelines that conflict with the content of these Regulations (if any).

2. The ArtAuction Project Management Board has the right to amend, supplement, or replace part or all of the content of these Regulations to suit actual circumstances and current legal regulations. All amendments and supplements to the Regulations will be publicly announced on the ArtAuction Platform homepage and/or via email sent to members before the effective date.

3. Members participating in the ArtAuction Platform are responsible for regularly updating the Regulations and related notices; continued use of the ArtAuction Platform services is understood as having read, understood, accepted, and committed to fully comply with these Regulations.

4. During implementation, if there are difficulties or obstacles, organizations and individuals may report to the ArtAuction Project Management Board for consideration, guidance, and appropriate adjustments.
