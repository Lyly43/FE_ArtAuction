ARTICLE 2. COMMITMENT REGARDING ASSET ORIGIN AND USE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE TECHNOLOGY

1. Party B commits that the auctioned asset is legally owned, not subject to disputes, complaints, not seized to secure judgment enforcement or to fulfill other civil obligations, and does not fall under cases prohibited from trading under applicable law.

2. Party B commits and assumes full responsibility for the accuracy, truthfulness, and legality of all information, documents, images, and descriptions of the artwork provided to Party A and posted on the ArtAuction system.

3. Party B commits that the artwork is a human-created product. In cases where the artwork uses, applies, or involves artificial intelligence (AI) technology, Party B is responsible for clearly and truthfully declaring the extent and scope of AI usage, ensuring no infringement of copyright, related rights, or other intellectual property rights.

4. Party B agrees and permits Party A to apply artificial intelligence technology (AI Verification) to scan, analyze, and evaluate image data and content related to the artwork to assist in verifying the origin, authenticity, and suitability of the artwork before and during the auction process.

5. In cases where the AI system and/or Party A's Administration Department detects or has reasonable grounds to believe that the artwork is counterfeit, infringes copyright, or is an AI-generated product but is misrepresented, recorded, or introduced as a painting/artwork created entirely by human manual methods, then:

a) Party A has the right to suspend, cancel the listing, stop the auction session (if ongoing), not recognize the auction result (if completed), and/or unilaterally terminate this Contract without compensation to Party B;

b) Party A has the right to review and apply measures against Party B's account, including but not limited to warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent suspension of the Seller account on the ArtAuction system;

c) Party B must bear full legal responsibility and compensate all affected parties (including Party A, Buyers, and related third parties, if any) in accordance with applicable law.

ARTICLE 3. SERVICE FEES AND PAYMENT METHODS

1. Listing fee for artwork on the ArtAuction platform:

Currently, Party A applies a free listing policy (or according to regulations and fee schedules published by Party A from time to time). Party A reserves the right to adjust this policy and will publicly announce it on the ArtAuction system.

2. Commission fee/Service fee for auction:

- Party B agrees to pay Party A a commission/service fee equivalent to ...% (in words: ... percent) calculated on the final winning bid price (Final Price) of the asset.

3. Payment method:

a) After the Buyer pays the full amount due for the auctioned asset, Party A will automatically deduct the commission/service fee mentioned in Clause 2 of this Article together with other fees (if any) according to the fee schedule published at the time of transaction;

b) The remaining amount after deducting the above fees will be transferred by Party A to Party B's Electronic Wallet (Wallet) on the ArtAuction system or through other payment methods notified and/or agreed with Party B (if any).

ARTICLE 4. RIGHTS AND OBLIGATIONS OF THE PARTIES

1. Rights and obligations of Party A

a) Organize and establish Auction Rooms on the ArtAuction platform to conduct auctions of Party B's assets in an objective, transparent manner, in accordance with legal regulations and Party A's internal regulations.

b) Ensure that the technical system and information technology infrastructure serving online auction organization operates stably and securely within the scope of capabilities, technical conditions, and excluding force majeure cases.

c) Protect the confidentiality of Party B's personal information and data according to privacy policies, personal data protection regulations, and relevant legal regulations, except when required to provide to competent state agencies or as required by law.

d) Support, receive, and resolve complaints related to the auction process, within the scope of Party A's authority and responsibilities.

e) Entitled to collect service fees in accordance with the agreement in this Contract and according to fee schedules and policies published on the ArtAuction system.

2. Rights and obligations of Party B

a) Provide complete, truthful, and accurate information, images, and documents related to the auctioned asset; assume legal responsibility and responsibility to Party A for all information provided.

b) May not unilaterally withdraw the asset from the auction session after the auction session has been opened, except in cases of force majeure and with Party A's written or valid electronic approval.

c) Package, preserve, and deliver the asset to the winning bidder according to the agreed time, location, and method or as instructed by Party A, ensuring the asset is in the same condition as described during the auction.

d) Cooperate with Party A in resolving complaints and disputes (if any) related to the auctioned asset.

e) Pay fully and on time all fees, charges, and related costs (if any) according to the provisions of this Contract and as notified by Party A.

ARTICLE 5. GENERAL PROVISIONS, DISPUTE RESOLUTION, AND CONTRACT EFFECTIVENESS

1. This Contract is established in the form of an Electronic Contract through the ArtAuction platform system. Party B's performance of actions such as "agree", "confirm", "accept" or equivalent actions on the system is considered as signing an Electronic Contract, having the same legal validity as a written contract under the Law on Electronic Transactions and related laws.

2. This Contract takes effect from the time Party B completes registration and confirms agreement with the terms of this Contract on the ArtAuction system and is recorded by Party A on the system.

3. Any amendments or supplements to this Contract (if any) shall be made through a Contract Appendix or through electronic forms supported by the ArtAuction system with confirmation from both Parties, and shall form an integral part of this Contract.

4. During the performance of this Contract, if disputes arise, the Parties shall prioritize resolution through negotiation and mediation in a spirit of cooperation and mutual respect. If negotiation fails within a reasonable time, either Party has the right to file a lawsuit at the competent People's Court in Da Nang City for resolution in accordance with the law.
